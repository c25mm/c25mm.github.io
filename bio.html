Imdontai, otherwise known as Dontai Anthony Ethridge

is known for being a youtuber since the early/mid 2010s. Dontai started his career doing reactions to films, video games, and hip hop music. His charismatic qualities were evident on screen and Dontai amassed a large, loyal fanbase. While there was no exact peak of Dontai's career, Dontai's growth on Youtube has been exponential, even leading him to make his own music that has gained hundreds of thousands of listens on spotify. Dontai has three youtube channels that have over 5 million subscribers combined and during the pandemic Dontai's streaming career has blown up on Twitch.
